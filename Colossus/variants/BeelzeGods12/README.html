BeelzeGods12 - Beelzebub12 with the Gods from Pantheon added, Version 1.02

Idea by Matt Roe, implemented by Clemens Katzer

Basically Beelzebub ...

Most of it comes from Beelzebub, so you should familiar with that, or look it up there.

... additionally, the following rules from Pantheon apply:

Titan gains teleport at 1000 points (like in Pantheon); Angel every 100 and Archangel every 500 is anyway same both in Beelzebub and Pantheon. (The additional acquirables from Beelzebub exist here too.)

The Titan-Druid-Knight-Priest-Estia line as in Pantheon does not exist.

As in Pantheon, the used-to-be summonables (Devil, Archdevil, Archmage, Fiend, Phantom and Death) are not summonable. Angels and Archangels are summonable. Since Beelzebub is unique (and hey, the variant is derived from Beelzebub ;-) , so it should be something special, Beelzebub is summonable.

Because there is no Mantio, those Pantheon gods are recruited in Hellmouth (numbers and recruiters are now totally different than in Pantheon):

All gods have magic missile ability

  • 2 Knights recruit Aphrodite 4 5 flyer
  • 3 Rangers recruit Apollo - 7 4 flyer
  • 2 Wraiths recruit Hermes - 12 3 flyer
  • 3 Basilisks recruit Dionysous - 10 3 flyer
  • 2 Phoenixes recruit Dimitra - 8 4
  • 2 Fiends recruit Estia - 12 3
  • 3 Golems recruit Ares - 11 3
  • 2 Hydrae recruit Poseidon - 14 3
  • 2 Unicorns recruit Hepfaestus - 11 4
  • 2 Manticores recruit Artemis - 15 3
  • 3 Mummies or 3 Lichs recruit Athena - 10 4
  • 2 Mammoths recruit Hades - 13 4
  • 2 Colossi recruit Hera - 12 3 flyer
  • 3 Lords or 4 DemiLords recruit Zeus - 13 4

Same as it is in Mantio, those Gods cannot recruit down - e.g. Apollo cannot recruit Rangers, and especially Estia cannot recruit down to Fiend, Devil or Imp.

Due to technical reasons (because now whole Hellmouth is marked as "cannot recruit down", also recruiting down from Beelzebub => Fiend => Devil is not possible (for Imp this does not matter, since one can get Imp in Hellmouth for anything).

(BTW: Same applies to Tower in all variants: even if "3 of any non-Lord" can recruit a Guardian, with Guardian you cannot recruit anything else than another Guardian; apart from that you can get the Tower base creatures because they can be recruited with reall "anything" - as long as the legion is not empty).

Note: Fiend is now DemiLord instead of Lord as in Beelzebub.
