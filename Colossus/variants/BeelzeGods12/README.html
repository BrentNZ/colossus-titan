BeelzeGods12 - Beelzebub12 with the Gods from Pantheon added

Idea by Matt Roe, implemented by Clemens Katzer

Basically Beelzebub ...

Most of it comes from Beelzebub, so you should familiar with that, or look it up there.

... additionally, the following rules from Pantheon apply:

Titan gains teleport at 1000 points (like in Pantheon); Angel every 100 and Archangel every 500 is anyway same both in Beelzebug and Pantheon.

The Titan-Druid-Knight-Priest-Estia line as in Pantheon does not exist.

As in Pantheon, normal lords (Angel and Archangel) are not summonable, only the Pantheon-Gods (the 12 Olympian Gods, plus the God of Underwold Hades and the reckless God Dionysous)

Because there is no Mantio, those gods are recruited in Hellmouth (same "numerical" conditions as in Pantheon):

  • 2 Colossus recruit Hera - 18 3 flyer
  • 2 Hydras recruit Hepfaestus - 20 2
  • 3 Hydras recruit Poseidon - 15 3
  • 3 Unicorns recruit Ares - 13 3
  • 2 Unicorns recruit Dimitra - 11 3
  • 3 Rangers recruit Aphrodite 6 4 flyer
  • 4 Rangers recruit Apolo - 7 4 rangestriker
  • 3 Gorgons recruit Hermes - 9 3 flyer
  • 4 Gorgons recruit Dionysous - 10 3 flyer, rangestriker
  • 2 Serpents recruit Artemis - 16 3 rangestriker
